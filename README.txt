This dataset was curated for publication as part of the manuscript in Moradi et al. (Detection of Alzheimer´s Disease Phenotype using Machine Learning Classifiers and early Electrophysiological Aberrations in the Hippocampus of the TgF344-AD Rat Model 
ISCIENCE, 2023). 
It contains LFPs originating from the right dorsal hippocampus collected from 9 rats during a linear track experiment.
